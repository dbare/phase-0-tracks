1. Withdrawing Money from ATM Algorithm

	-locate your ATM card
	-insert card into ATM
		-make sure magnetic strip is face down
		-make sure magnetic strip is on the right side of the card
		-remove card
	-enter your 4 digit PIN number
		- press enter
	-select “withdraw”
	-select “checking”
	-select the amount you would like to withdraw
		-accept transaction fees
	-Pull money out of ATM after its despensed
