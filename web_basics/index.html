Pied Piper

Pied Piper is a fictional compression company from the HBO show Silicon Vally. Basically, the main character has a compression algorithm that is so much better than everyone elses, that he is launched into the insane world of the tech industry. The show is both original and hilarious, I would recommend whatching it.
